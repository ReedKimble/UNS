# **AI Attribution & Authorship Statement**

This document clarifies the role of AI assistance in the creation and development of the **Universal Number Set (UNS)** and establishes the authorship, origin, and nature of its mathematical and conceptual framework.

---

## **1. Role of AI Assistants**

The UNS project made use of modern AI-assisted tools, including:

* **ChatGPT 5.1** — for drafting, refining, structuring documents, suggesting mathematical formulations, and assisting with expression and organization under strict constraints defined by the human author.
* **GitHub Copilot** — for code scaffolding, syntax refinement, and development support within the author’s established design patterns.

These systems served exclusively as **assistive tools**, participating in the design process under human direction.

Their contribution is best described as collaborative support — similar to working with a reasoning partner, research assistant, or technical editor:

* offering structured suggestions
* helping formalize language rules
* offering ways to express functional or operator relationships
* exploring the implications of axioms provided by the human author

However, **all high-level concepts, foundational principles, constraints, axioms, and the overall mathematical direction of UNS came from the human author.**

---

## **2. Human Authorship & Conceptual Origin of UNS**

The **Universal Number Set** was **conceptually originated and directed entirely by the human author**, including:

* the defining idea of numbers as **distributions over microstates**
* the requirement of **normalized value distributions**
* the distinction between **microstates**, **states**, and **readout functions**
* the principles behind **dimensional equivalence**
* the generalized notion of **lifted operations**
* the introduction, purpose, and constraints governing **novel values**
* the philosophical position that **classical numbers are special cases within a broader numerical universe**
* the overall architecture, structure, semantics, and internal logic of UNS
* the creation of the **`.unse` expression language and file format**

AI assistance did help refine aspects of the mathematical structure within the boundaries defined by the author — such as suggesting ways to formally represent per-microstate values, lifted operators, and composed transformations. These suggestions were always considered, interpreted, and shaped by the human author, and were included only when consistent with the governing axioms and conceptual goals.

A precise characterization is:

> **UNS was invented by the human author; AI assisted in expressing and structuring the mathematical language.**

## **4. Summary**

* **UNS was conceptualized, defined, and originated by the human author.**
* **AI assistants provided structural, organizational, and mathematical expression support**, always within human-defined constraints.
* **AI did not invent or originate the mathematical concepts, philosophical principles, or architectural structure of UNS.**
* The relationship is best summarized as:
  **human-led design with AI-assisted formulation and refinement.**
