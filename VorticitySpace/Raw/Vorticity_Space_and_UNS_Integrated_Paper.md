## Abstract

This paper unifies the *Vorticity Space Hypothesis* with the mathematical framework of the **Unified Number Space (UNS)**, yielding a complete, empirically actionable theory of physical law. The resulting model derives all known domains of physics—quantum, relativistic, thermodynamic, and cosmological—from a single invariant:  
\[

\]  
This completeness condition replaces the need for separate postulates of energy, spacetime, or substance. From it emerge asymmetry, dimensionality, curvature, and conservation as mathematical necessities. Dark matter, dark energy, and the observer effect all arise as self-consistent features of the normalization manifold. The theory provides a closed logical framework reconciling Gödel incompleteness, quantum superposition, and general relativity under one relational principle.
